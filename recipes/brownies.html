Brownies

Description

These simple brownies can be made in 35 minutes and can yield up to 16 pieces for you to share with others, or eat by yourself with a gallon of vanilla ice cream sitting on your couch watching a rom-com. Either way this quick and delicious dessert will satisfy your taste buds and can be paired with out desserts.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat an 8x8-inch metal baking dish with butter (not the butter used in making the brownies).
  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and let cool. Whisk in cocoa powder, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir in eggs, 1 at a time until incorporated. Add flour and stir until just combined. Scrape mixture into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack before cutting.
